Aven

Tunable‑white • DALI‑2 ready • Indoor

Request Quote Ask a Question

Aven pairs architectural simplicity with DALI‑2 control. Every unit ships with tunable white as standard, so scenes feel natural from sunrise to evening.

  • Tunable‑white out of the box
  • Compatible with DALI‑2 and Control4/Lutron via DALI gateways
  • Available in wired or wireless (Zigbee→DALI bridge) configurations

Optics

  • CCT Range: 2700–5000K (options available)
  • CRI: 90+ (typ.)
  • Beam Options: Flood / Spot (specify)

Electrical

  • Input: 120–277V (driver dependent)
  • Control: DALI‑2 (TW)
  • Wireless Option: Zigbee→DALI bridge

Mechanical

  • Trim: Round / Square
  • Cutout: see size options
  • Finish: White / Black / Custom

Lead time typically 2–4 weeks depending on configuration. Items are built to order. Returns accepted within 30 days for unused products in original packaging; RMA required.
