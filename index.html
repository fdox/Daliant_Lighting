Builder Packages

From plans to preprogrammed keypads—we simplify lighting for your entire build.

Our builder packages streamline the electrical rough-in process by eliminating complex wiring plans. By standardizing DALI control across the entire home, we make it faster and easier to install smart lighting — all with fewer wires, less labor, and more design flexibility. We handle fixture configuration, keypad planning, and control system prep, so your team can stay focused on building, not debugging lighting systems.
